## Usage Notes

- All article images are used as hero images in article cards, article detail views, task cards, and feature screens
- Icons are used as small thumbnails in task lists, conservation projects, and feature grid cards
- Onboarding images must be full-screen resolution (at least 1170x2532 for iPhone 14 Pro)
- The header image is stretched and clipped to fill the header area across all screens
- All images are loaded via `Image("name")` from the asset catalog
- No hardcoded strings are used for image names — they come from JSON data files
